As a part of community, we conduct regular sports competitions and activities encouraging the members to be "the active members." We also offer several facilities.

Facilities:


  • Cricket Ground:
     We offer our community a well equipped International Standard Cricket Ground. It is surrounded by seating arrangements. It will be open for almost 19 hrs(6:00 AM to 11:00 PM) except on monday.
  • Gymnasium:
     We have Three Gyms. Our largest gym, i.e., GymKindle is highly spacious with a capacity of 75+ people at a same time. It is open for all the Gentlemen and Ladies of the community. The other two gyms, i.e., GymPrayaas and GymShourya are of capacity of 30+ members each and are separately available for Women and Men respectively.
  • Football Ground:
    We also offer a well set Football ground.
  • Athletic Ground & Track:
     We have an Athletic ground suitable for Sports like Javelin Throw , Shortput , Hammer throw. That Ground is covered by Running track with the coverage of 500mtrs.
  • Community Park
     We have very calm Park where anyone can come and spend time with family and people.
